Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I register for an auction?

To register for an auction, please visit our website and create an account. Once logged in, you can browse and register for upcoming auctions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. You can find more details on accepted payment methods during the auction registration process.

Are there any additional fees on top of the auction price?

Additional fees may apply, such as buyer's premium or transaction fees. These details are usually outlined in the auction terms and conditions or displayed during the bidding process.

How can I track my auction winnings or purchases?

Once you've successfully won an auction or made a purchase, you should receive a confirmation email. You can also log in to your account on our website to view your purchase history and track your items.
